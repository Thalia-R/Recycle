GUIDED TOURS 2021

The guided bicycle tours season starts Mai 18th and ends August 20th.

Tours Monday-Thursday :

  • 10.00 - Oslo Central station/ B/ T
  • 11.30 - Re-Cycle office/ T
  • 15.30 - Oslo Central station/ P

Tours Friday-Sunday :

  • 10.20 - Oslo Central station/ B/ T
  • 14.30 - Re-Cycle office/ T
  • 12.30 - Oslo Central station/ P

Which tour should you take?

  • B = Beginners - small, easy routes (1 hour) - suites all ages.
  • T = Trained - longer, medium hard routes (3 hours) - suits thoose who is used to cycling
  • P = Pro - hard route with hills (4 hours) - suits thoose who wants exploring with training
